# 7 · Why Choose GEVINO Instead of a Traditional PLC?

> From the GEVA Elettronica blog — **[read the original article](https://shop.gevaelettronica.it/en/blog/post/1-gevino-vs-plc-quale-scegliere-nel-2026)**
> (posted 06/18/2026, by Giorgio Evangelista).

For decades, PLCs have been the standard solution for industrial automation. Today, however,
advances in embedded electronics and artificial intelligence offer new approaches that are more
affordable, flexible, and powerful.

GEVINO was designed with this goal in mind: providing a modern industrial control platform that is
open, versatile, and easy to develop.

---

## Lower initial costs

A traditional PLC often requires:

- A dedicated CPU
- Expansion modules
- Proprietary programming software
- Additional licenses
- Paid updates or subscriptions

With GEVINO, the hardware is complete and the development software is free. No annual licenses or
software subscriptions are required.

---

## AI-assisted programming

Traditional PLCs are typically programmed using specialized languages such as Ladder Logic,
Function Block Diagrams (FBD), or Structured Text.

GEVINO uses C/C++, one of the world's most widely adopted programming languages for embedded
systems.

Today, Artificial Intelligence tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, and GitHub Copilot can generate,
explain, and improve C/C++ code, significantly reducing development time.

In many cases, users can implement advanced automation functions without being experts in
industrial programming.

---

## Higher performance

Many entry-level PLCs operate with scan cycles measured in milliseconds.

A GEVINO controller based on the SAMD21 microcontroller can execute operations hundreds or even
thousands of times faster, enabling:

- High-speed data acquisition
- Real-time control applications
- Advanced mathematical processing
- Simultaneous management of multiple communication protocols

---

## Greater development freedom

Traditional PLC platforms often lock users into a specific manufacturer's ecosystem.

With GEVINO, developers can:

- Use open-source libraries
- Integrate cloud services
- Communicate through Ethernet, Wi-Fi, MQTT, Modbus, and many other protocols
- Fully customize the firmware

This provides complete control over the project and eliminates vendor lock-in.

---

## Why choose a SAMD21-based GEVINO?

The SAMD21 microcontroller offers an excellent balance between cost, power consumption, and
performance.

Key advantages include:

- 32-bit ARM Cortex-M0+ architecture
- Up to 48 MHz clock speed
- Significantly more memory than traditional 8-bit microcontrollers
- 12-bit analog-to-digital converters
- Multiple integrated hardware peripherals
- Very low power consumption
- High reliability for industrial applications

For automation, remote monitoring, telemetry, and data acquisition systems, the SAMD21 delivers
more than enough processing power without the complexity and cost of larger platforms.

---

## When a PLC may still be the better choice

PLCs continue to offer advantages in some situations:

- Existing staff already trained on PLC platforms
- Industry-specific certification requirements
- Corporate standardization policies
- Centralized vendor support

In highly traditional industrial environments, a PLC may still be the most straightforward
solution.

---

## Conclusion

GEVINO is not designed to imitate a traditional PLC. It was created to provide a more open,
affordable, and flexible approach to industrial automation.

Thanks to AI-assisted programming, the absence of software licensing costs, and the capabilities of
the SAMD21 microcontroller, GEVINO represents a compelling alternative for many automation, remote
control, and industrial monitoring applications.
